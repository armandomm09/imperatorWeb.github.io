FRC Team Imperator 5887 is a high school robotics team based in Tecnologico de Monterrey Campus Puebla. Founded in 2016, our team not only competes in the First Robotics Competition, but we also focus on inspiring youger generations to pursue success in STEAM areas regardless of their age, gender and origin. As a team, we believe that science is for everyone! Membership is open to all high school students in Tecnologico de Monterrey Campus Puebla. FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global organization inspiring a love for science and technology in youth. Through captivating robotics programs, students design, build, and program robots, cultivating vital STEM skills. This hands-on experience fosters teamwork, innovation, and a commitment to problem-solving. Joining a FIRST team offers not just technical growth but also a close-knit community, providing support and encouragement for a lifelong journey in STEM.
